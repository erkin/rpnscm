* RPNSCM v0.7
A primitive RPN calculator written in CHICKEN Scheme.

** Note
    This is the development branch for the alist-based version.
    It is exact-integer-only until I implement precision.

** Eggs
   * =srfi-13= (for =string-pad= & =string-tokenize=)
   * =args= (for =args=)

** Usage
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
csc rpnscm.system
./rpnscm -e "EXPRESSION"
#+END_EXAMPLE
    
*** Example
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
% rpnscm -e "4 3 % 7 7 * * 2 / 10 -"
Input: (4 3 % 7 7 * * 2 / 10 -)
 0: (4 3 % 7 7 * * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ ()
 1: (3 % 7 7 * * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (4)
 2: (% 7 7 * * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (4 3)
 3: (7 7 * * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (1)
 4: (7 * * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (1 7)
 5: (* * 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (1 7 7)
 6: (* 2 / 10 -) ⇒ (7 7)
 7: (2 / 10 -) ⇒ (49)
 8: (/ 10 -) ⇒ (49 2)
 9: (10 -) ⇒ (24)
10: (-) ⇒ (24 10)
11: () ⇒ (14)
Output: (14)
#+END_EXAMPLE
